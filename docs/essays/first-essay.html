The Built Environment & Human Existence

“Egotism itself, which is so necessary to a proper sense of human dignity, is entirely the result of indoor life.” (Wilde, The Decay of Lying, 4)

I observed a tree in the Cambridge Commons while considering the sentence...

Humans, like all other animals, once occupied a world that was indifferent to us...

The indoor world we have crafted makes complete sense in its own terms...

So even though it was long overdue, I am really grateful to have taken this first step, so to speak.
